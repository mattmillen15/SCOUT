Assessment summary

This assessment of ecorp.local returns an overall danger score of 100/100 (CRITICAL RISK), taken as the worst of four category scores — weakest is Anomaly at 100/100. SCOUT raised 22 findings (8 critical, 9 high).

On the CMMI maturity scale the domain sits at Level 1 — Initial. AD security is unpredictable and ad-hoc. Instantly-exploitable issues remain — an attacker on the LAN can likely reach Domain Admin today.

Most urgent: the environment exposes recoverable GPP passwords in SYSVOL, DCSync rights granted to non-admins, an ESC1-exploitable certificate template and WDigest cleartext credential caching — each a realistic, low-cost path to domain compromise that should be remediated first.

Methodology & scope

Tool. SCOUT v2.0.0 — an offline, Linux-based Active Directory security assessment over LDAP/LDAPS/SMB covering four risk categories (Anomaly, Privileged, Stale, Trust) with classic and modern-escalation coverage (RBCD, constrained delegation, ADCS ESC, privileged SID-history, computer accounts in privileged groups).
